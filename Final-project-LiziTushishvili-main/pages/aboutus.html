About Us

At #15 Public School of City of Rustavi, we believe that education is the key to unlocking the limitless potential within each and every student. Established with a vision to foster holistic development and ignite the spark of curiosity, our school is a nurturing ground for young minds to thrive and become lifelong learners.


Our Philosophy:
Our educational philosophy revolves around the core principles of providing a safe, inclusive, and stimulating learning environment. We strive to inspire our students to become critical thinkers, problem solvers, and compassionate individuals who are equipped to make a positive impact on the world around them.

Excellence in Education:
We are committed to delivering excellence in education through a dynamic and comprehensive curriculum that not only meets academic standards but also encourages creativity, innovation, and practical application of knowledge. Our dedicated team of educators is passionate about teaching and works diligently to tailor their approach to meet the unique needs and learning styles of each student.

A Supportive Community:
We recognize the importance of a strong and supportive community. Our school is not just a place of learning; it is a close-knit family of teachers, students, parents, and staff, all working collaboratively to create a nurturing atmosphere. We believe that strong partnerships between home and school enhance the overall educational experience for our students.

Holistic Development:
We understand that academic excellence is just one aspect of a well-rounded individual. Therefore, we emphasize holistic development by providing a wide range of co-curricular activities, sports, arts, and cultural programs. These activities empower our students to discover their passions, build self-confidence, and develop essential life skills.

State-of-the-Art Facilities:
Our campus boasts state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, a well-stocked library. These facilities create an enriching and conducive environment for our students to explore and excel in their areas of interest.

Inclusive Education:
We celebrate diversity and believe in creating an inclusive and nurturing space for all students. Our inclusive education practices ensure that every student receives the support and guidance they need to reach their full potential, regardless of their background or abilities.

Parent Involvement:
We strongly believe that parent involvement is vital to a child's education. We encourage open communication and regular interactions between parents and teachers to foster a strong partnership in nurturing the academic and personal growth of each student.
